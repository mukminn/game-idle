## üìä Design Principles

1. **Player Agency Through Strategy** - Players control progression, not combat
2. **Meaningful Progression** - Every upgrade feels impactful
3. **Fair Monetization** - Pay for convenience, not power
4. **Scalable Architecture** - Designed for years of content updates
5. **Anti-Pay-to-Win** - Skill and strategy matter more than spending
